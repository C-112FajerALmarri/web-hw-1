ludo star

take turns in a clockwise order; highest
throw of the die starts. Each throw,
the player decides which piece to move.
A piece simply moves in a clockwise direction around
the track given by the number thrown. If no piece
legally move according to the number thrown,
play passes to the next player.

  1. Place all of your pieces onto the corresponding color pocket.
  2. Decide who goes first by rolling the die.
  3. Roll a 6 to bring your first pawn onto the board
  4. Roll again to determine how many spaces to move your first pawn
  5. End your turn and pass the die to the next player after you move a pawn
  6. Place another pawn on the board if you roll another 6
  7. Continue to roll at the beginning of each turn and move your pawns.
  8. Capture an opponent’s pawn by landing on the same space
  9. Get more than 1 of your pieces on the same space to make a block
  10. Land on the same space as an opponent’s pawn to move past it
  11. Move your pawns around the board going towards your home stretch
  12. Avoid spaces with special markings on them
  13. Roll the exact number needed to get each pawn onto the home base
  14. Reach the home space with all 4 of your pawns first to win the game
